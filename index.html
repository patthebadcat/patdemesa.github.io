About Me

I've always been a data-oriented person. I like creating meaningful and useful insights from the numbers. I love how it can take you from one thing to another without you even realizing it—and then all of a sudden you have answers to questions that you didn't even know existed and I also love finding new ways to present information so that people can make better choices for themselves.

My goal with these works is to help people find their own answers by providing them with the tools and information they need to do it on their own. I'm a big believer in the power of information to change lives. I believe that the best way to understand the world around us is by analyzing data and making it accessible to everyone.
